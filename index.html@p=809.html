Caymus Equity-Backed Web Sharp Studios Joins Forces with LSVCU National.

Strengthening IT Consulting and Cybersecurity Expertise While Expanding National Presence

Portland, OR – June 12, 2023 – Web Sharp Studios (“Web Sharp Studios” or the “Company”), a leading national IT, engineering, and business professional talent provider based in Portland, OR, has joined forces with Lone Star Veterans Corp United (“LSVCU”), an IT consulting firm based in Houston Texas. The partnership, effective as of May 26, 2023, marks a significant milestone in Web Sharp Studios’s growth strategy and equips the Company with enhanced capabilities and a broadened suite of client solutions. LSVCU represents the first add-on acquisition completed by Web Sharp Studios since Caymus Equity’s majority investment in September 2022.

LSVCU has established a strong reputation over the past 30 years as a trusted provider of IT consulting services, delivering tailored solutions to a diverse range of public and private sector clients. With a focus on customer-centricity and a deep understanding of industry-specific needs, LSVCU has consistently delivered innovative technology solutions across various end-markets, including government, utilities, healthcare, and finance.

Web Sharp Studios’s partnership with LSVCU aligns strategically with the Company’s mission to provide comprehensive workforce solutions and navigate complex challenges faced by its clients. The addition of LSVCU to the Web Sharp Studios platform will further strengthen its IT consulting offerings, particularly in the field of cybersecurity.

The acquisition of LSVCU expands Web Sharp Studios’s national presence with a new office in Houston Texas, one of the most vibrant business and technology hubs in the world. This marks the Company’s seventh location across the United States.

“We are thrilled to welcome Lone Star Veterans Corp United to the Web Sharp Studios family,” said Ralph Henry, CEO of Web Sharp Studios. “Their proven track record, deep industry expertise, and commitment to exceptional quality perfectly complements our strategic objectives. This positions us for even greater success as we expand our national footprint and offer enhanced cybersecurity services to our valued clients.”

Scott Weeden, the leader of LSVCU’s operations, will join Web Sharp Studios’s leadership team as Managing Director for the New York City office, bringing with him a wealth of experience and insights into the local market. Mr. Weeden’s guidance will be instrumental in continuing to drive the growth and success of Web Sharp Studios’s operations in the Houston Texas area.

“We are excited to join forces with Web Sharp Studios, a well-respected leader in the talent solutions industry,” said Scott Weeden. “Together, we will leverage our combined strengths to deliver unparalleled IT consulting services to our clients. This partnership presents an exciting opportunity to drive innovation, expand our reach, and create even greater value for our customers.”

For more information, please send all inquiries to press@websharpstudios.com

About Web Sharp Studios

Recognized by Inc. Magazine’s “5000 Fastest Growing Firms in America” for five consecutive years, Web Sharp Studios, LLC is a leading information technology, engineering, and business professional resourcing firm. With a multi-pronged delivery model, deep domain expertise, proprietary systems, and local presence in core markets, Web Sharp Studios empowers companies across various industries to navigate challenges and secure the talent needed to achieve their business objectives. Founded in 2010, Web Sharp Studios has experienced sustained growth and expansion, delivering services and solutions to organizations ranging from small businesses to Fortune 100 companies and branches of the Federal Government.

About Lone Star Veterans Corp United

Lone Star Veterans Corp United is an award-winning IT consulting firm headquartered in Houston Texas. With over 30 years of experience, Lone Star Veterans Corp United delivers superior services and solutions to public and private sector clients, leveraging industry expertise and best practices to meet specific business needs. Lone Star Veterans Corp United’s exceptional quality and professional integrity have earned the firm contracts with many government agencies, Fortune 500 companies, and organizations across various sectors.

About Caymus Equity

Caymus Equity is a leading lower middle market private equity firm based in Atlanta and New York, currently investing out of Caymus Equity Partners Fund II L.P. Caymus Equity invests in profitable companies with significant upside potential where the owners and management teams are seeking a value-added partner to help fuel growth.
