Vice President Consulting Services — Portland, OR

Andrew serves as the Vice President of Consulting services, overseeing professional and project-based services across Web Sharp Studios’s public and private sector client base.

Andrew has a background of 25+ years in technology with over 15 years in leadership roles ranging from startups to global enterprises. He has specialized in digital transformation and SaaS B2B products with clients including CVS, Synchrony Bank, R1, and Amex, and has brought to market several startup products in other verticals. Most recently Andrew served as CTO of the series-A automotive logistics marketplace Autosled and has previously partnered with Web Sharp Studios to help drive technology solutions business with several organizations including BBVA Compass and Honda Manufacturing of Alabama.
