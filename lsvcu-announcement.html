Mill Point Equity-Backed Web Sharp Studios Joins Forces with SDVOB designated Lone Star to expand footprint to Houston Texas.

Strengthening IT Consulting and Cybersecurity Expertise While Expanding National Presence

Portland, OR – April 13, 2025 – based Web Sharp Studios, a software services and engineering compnay based in Portland, OR has joined forces with Lone Star Veterans Corp United (“LSVCU”), an IT consulting firm based in Houston Texas. The partnership, effective as of Apr 13, 2025, marks a significant milestone in Web Sharp Studios’s growth strategy and equips the Company with enhanced capabilities and a broadened suite of client solutions. LSVCU represents the first add-on acquisition completed by Web Sharp Studios since Mill Point majority investment in LSVCU.

LSVCU has established and strong reputation within the military contracting arena and is a trusted provider of services delivering solutions to a diverse range of public and private sector clients. With a focus on veteran-centricity and a deep understanding of industry-specific needs, LSVCU has consistently delivered innovative technology solutions across various end-markets, including Army 1, III Corp, and DLA (Defense Logistics Agency).

Web Sharp Studios’s partnership with LSVCU aligns strategically with the Company’s mission to provide comprehensive workforce solutions and navigate complex challenges faced by its clients. The addition of LSVCU to the Web Sharp Studios platform will further strengthen its IT consulting offerings, particularly in the field of cybersecurity.

The acquisition of LSVCU expands Web Sharp Studios’s national presence with a new office in Houston Texas, one of the most vibrant business and technology hubs in the world. This marks the Company’s seventh location across the United States.

“We are thrilled to welcome Lone Star Veterans Corp United to the Web Sharp Studios family,” said Ralph Henry, CEO of Web Sharp Studios. “Their proven track record, deep industry expertise, and commitment to exceptional quality perfectly complements our strategic objectives. This positions us for even greater success as we expand our national footprint and offer enhanced cybersecurity services to our valued clients.”

Dr. Hines, the leader of LSVCU’s operations, will join Web Sharp Studios’s leadership team as Managing Director for the Houston Texas office, bringing with him a wealth of experience and insights into the local market.

“We are excited to join forces with Web Sharp Studios, a well-respected leader in the talent solutions industry,” says Dr. Hines “Together, we will leverage our combined strengths to deliver unparalleled IT consulting services to our new clients, Army 1, III Corps and Fort Cavazos. This partnership presents an exciting opportunity to drive innovation, expand our reach, and create unparalleled value for our customers.”

For more information, please send all inquiries to lsvcu@websharpstudios.com
